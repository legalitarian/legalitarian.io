Privacy Policy

Privacy is crucial to protecting the rights of the people, and to getting true and honest reports when someone's rights are violated. Legalitarian products will never collect information that could be used to identify a user, except when the user submits it directly to us. We will not share a user's identifiable information without their consent. All information submitted by a user is encrypted in transit and at rest, and identifying and contact information like a user's name or email address are stored separately from other information they submit.

Legalitarian will never collect information entered into forms on its websites except when the user submits that information using a button in the form.

Users may choose to make their identifying and contact information available to a specific Legalitarian partner. That information is stored securely and separately from the other information they submitted. However, when user-submitted information is provided to Legalitarian partners, the identifying information for a user who chose to share it is provided alongside the other information they submitted.

Tracking Use of Legalitarian Products

Legalitarian may collect information about the use of its products. This will never include personal identifiable information nor tracking or marketing IDs designed to track an individual user's activity across multiple websites or over time.

It can include actions taken by a user during a session using a Legalitarian website, including but not limited to: the order and number of pages visited, buttons that were clicked, the amount of time a user spent taking an action, scroll position mouse cursor movement, and focus on page elements.

The IP address from which requests were made may be collected in an attempt to identify misuse of Legalitarian websites by bots. This information may be encrypted and stored in connection with a suspected fraudulent form submission. If the form submission is determined to be genuine, the encrypted IP address will be erased. User IP addresses will never be shared with any of Legalitarian's partners.
